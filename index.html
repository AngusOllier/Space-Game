Moon Shot Navigator

The Moon takes about 27 days to orbit the Earth.

In this game,I have speeded the rockets up so instead of taking 3 days for the rocket to get to the moon it will take 3 seconds and instead of taking 27 days for the moon to get around the Earth it will take 14 seconds

Note:The moon is 384,400 kilometers from Earth so no wonder it takes 3 days to get to the moon

How to Play

Your mission to get to the moon but you have to time it right otherwise your explorers will be destroyed
